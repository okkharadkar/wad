Kashi Vishwanath Temple, Varanasi

The Kashi Vishwanath Temple, one of the 12 Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva, is located in the spiritual capital of India â€“ Varanasi. It's a major pilgrimage site for Hindus, and the Ganga Aarti performed here is mesmerizing.

Golden Temple, Amritsar

The Harmandir Sahib, also known as the Golden Temple, is the most important pilgrimage site of Sikhism. It is open to people of all religions and offers free meals to thousands every day through its Langar kitchen.

Ram Mandir, Ayodhya

The Ram Mandir in Ayodhya is a recently built grand temple dedicated to Lord Ram at his birthplace. The temple stands as a symbol of cultural unity and devotion, and has become a major attraction in Uttar Pradesh.

Kedarnath Temple, Uttarakhand

Kedarnath is one of the holiest Hindu shrines and part of the Char Dham Yatra. Located at an altitude of 3,583 meters in the Garhwal Himalayas, the temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and surrounded by snow-capped peaks.
